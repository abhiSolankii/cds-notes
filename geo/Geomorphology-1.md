#### 8. **Geological and Seismological Concepts**

- Earth's interior studied via **direct** (deep drilling, volcanism) and **indirect** (seismology) methods.
- Seismic waves:
  - **Primary (P) waves:** Travel through solids, liquids, and gases.
  - **Secondary (S) waves:** Travel only through solids.
- Earth's layers affect seismic wave velocity and direction, creating zones like the **shadow zone** where waves are not detected.
- Definitions:
  - **Focus:** Origin point of an earthquake inside Earth.
  - **Epicenter:** Surface point directly above focus.
  - **Isoseismal lines:** Lines joining points of equal seismic intensity.
